Privacy Policy for Item Donations

1. Introduction

At DonorConnect, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: This includes name, email address, contact information, addresses and any other details needed to facilitate donations or delivery.
  • Item Details: Information about donated items, including images, descriptions, expiry dates and conditions.
  • Usage Data: Data on user activity.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Facilitating Donations: To connect donors with recipients and ensure the smooth processing of donations.
  • Communications: To send updates, notifications, and information relevant to your activity on the platform.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze usage trends and improve platform functionality and user experience.

4. Sharing of Information

  • Recipients/Donors: To facilitate item exchange, we may share contact details with recipients or donors directly involved in the transaction.
  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist in delivery or analytics, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, we may disclose information to authorities for legal or security reasons.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of your personal information held by us.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccuracies in your information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page, and users will be notified where applicable.
